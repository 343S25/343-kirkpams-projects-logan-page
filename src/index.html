Welcome to the Listening Gallery

The Listening Gallery aims to help people keep track of their favorite songs in a separated environment in the form of their own personal art gallery. A lot of current playlist applications donâ€™t display the interesting album covers, or at least not big enough to really see sometimes. Instead of having a bland list of songs, it can look more like an art museum displaying the creativity of publishing music beyond the beats and lyrics. It is not a super important issue to solve, but some people who have a deeper appreciation of presentation may have a desire to have their songs displayed with a cover that helps visually display their song. The purpose is to have fun with it, and make a musical portfolio that can be edited between music and color. This gives someone the power to express themselves through their music tastes, as being able to convey our interest in this form of art can be helpful in interacting with others and make us feel involved. This is supposed to be a space where the effort put into the cover can be appreciated to its fullest extent; an image that can be heard...literally.

Instructions

  1. Verify your access to Spotify
  2. Get your access token
  3. Open Spotify: play any song then pause it
  4. Go to the Listening Gallery
  5. Search for songs and edit the layout
  6. Clicking the cover plays/pauses the song
